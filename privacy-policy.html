Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2025
Last Updated: June 9, 2025

Welcome to WeatherPower. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information (only if provided): Name, email address, admin credentials.
  • Location Data: To provide accurate radar and alerts, if you give permission.
  • Device Information: Browser, OS, screen size, etc.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, feature usage, timestamps.
  • Custom Alerts: Alert data submitted through the admin system.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver real-time radar and weather services.
  • Send alerts and notifications.
  • Improve functionality and performance.
  • Protect against abuse or unauthorized access.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers (e.g., Firebase, ElevenLabs, RainViewer, Mapbox, OpenWeather).
  • Legal Authorities if required by law.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics to improve your experience. You may opt out via your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We use modern encryption and security practices to protect your data, especially admin login credentials.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If discovered, such data will be deleted.

7. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, delete, or limit use of your data. Contact us to make a request.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this page at any time. Revisions will include a new effective date.
